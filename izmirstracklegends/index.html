Izmir's Track Legends

  Izmir's Track Legends(ITL) offers player to drive unique cars' owned by locals. Goal of the game is for the player to try to get the best lap time. However ITL is yet to offer a "Leaderboard" system. Currently ITL has 8 cars to drive but I aim to increase the number in the future. For now ITL can only be played on Windows platform.
