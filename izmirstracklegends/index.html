İzmir's Track Legends

Time-Trial Based Racing Game

Summary

      “İzmir’s Track Legends” video game is made by one developer. My development motivation for this game was creating a digital copy of a local track in İzmir, as well as demonstrating my skills and knowledge all in one project.

Developer

Eren GARİPAĞAOĞLU

Fullstack Developer | Web & 3D Designer | IT Solutions

Features

  • Track checkpoints and 1:1 realtime time tracking for laptime.
  • Analog & digital gauges for speed & rpm.
  • Tire smoke physics after tires start slipping.
  • Vehicles have their own sound effects as well as gauges.
  • Manual & automatic gearbox(transmission) systems while driving.
  • FWD, RWD, 4X4 drivetrain physics.

In-Game Content
  • 1 Racing Track: İzmir Park Racing Circuit, with detailed environment.
  • 18 Cars
